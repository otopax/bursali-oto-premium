In this article, we consider the third-generation KIA Picanto (JA), available from 2018 to the present. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of KIA Picanto 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout) and relay.
