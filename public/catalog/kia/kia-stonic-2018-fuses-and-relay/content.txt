The subcompact crossover KIA Stonic is available from 2018 to the present. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of KIA Stonic 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout) and relay.

Fuse Layout KIA Stonic 2018-2019…




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the KIA Stonic are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses “POWER OUTLET” and “C/LIGHTER”).
