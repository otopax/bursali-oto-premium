In this article, we consider the second-generation KIA Picanto (TA), produced from 2012 to 2017. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of KIA Picanto 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout KIA Picanto 2012-2017




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the KIA Picanto are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses “P/OUTLET” (Power Outlet) and “C/LIGHT” (Cigarette lighter)).
