In this article, we consider the third generation KIA Carens (RP), available from 2014 to the present. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of KIA Carens 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout) and relay.

Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuse “POWER OUTLET 1” (Cigarette lighter, rear power outlet)) and in the Engine compartment fuse box (fuse “P/OUTLET 2” (Power Outlet Ralay 2)).
