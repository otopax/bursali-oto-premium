The Mitsubishi Lancer X was produced from 2007 to 2017. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Mitsubishi Lancer X 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2106 and 2017, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Mitsubishi Lancer X 2008-2017




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Mitsubishi Lancer X are the fuses #13 (Cigarette lighter / Accessory socket), #19 (Accessory socket) and #23 (115v power outlet) in the Instrument panel fuse box.
