Fuse Layout Mitsubishi Outlander Sport / ASX 2011-2019




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Mitsubishi Outlander Sport / ASX are the fuses #13 (Cigarette lighter / Accessory socket), #19 (Accessory socket) and #23 (115v power outlet) in the Instrument panel fuse box.
