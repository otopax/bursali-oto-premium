Fuse Layout Mitsubishi Outlander 2007-2013



Information from the owner’s manuals of 2010, 2011, 2012 and 2013 is used. The location and function of fuses in cars produced at other times may differ.

Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Mitsubishi Outlander are the fuses #13 (Cigarette lighter / Accessory socket), #19 (Accessory socket) and #23 (115v power outlet) in the Instrument panel fuse box.

Fuse Box Location
Passenger Compartment
It is located behind the cover to the left of the steering wheel.

A – Main fuse block
Open the personal compartment and pull to remove it.


B – Sub fuse block
Remove the personal compartment in the same way that you would with the main fuse block, use the fuse remover to pry loose the 2 clips (E) on the bottom cover.


Engine Compartment
The fuse box is located in the engine compartment (left-side).
