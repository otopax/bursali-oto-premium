Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Mitsubishi Outlander 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, and 2021, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Mitsubishi Outlander 2014-2021




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses: #9 (2014-2016) or #7 (2017-2019) (12v power outlet), and #23 (Cigarette lighter / 12v power outlet) in the Instrument panel fuse box.

Fuse Box Location
Passenger Compartment
The fuse box is located in the instrument panel (on the driver’s side) to the left of the steering wheel, behind the cover.
A – Main fuse block; B – Sub fuse block.
Engine Compartment
It is located in the engine compartment (left-side).
A – Main fuse block; B – Sub fuse block (if equipped).
