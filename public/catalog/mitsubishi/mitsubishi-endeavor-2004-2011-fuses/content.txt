The mid-size crossover Mitsubishi Endeavor was produced from 2004 to 2011. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Mitsubishi Endeavor 2010 and 2011, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Mitsubishi Endeavor 2004-2011



Information from the owner’s manuals of 2010 and 2011 is used. The location and function of fuses in cars produced at other times may differ.

Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Mitsubishi Endeavor are the fuses #9 (Power outlet), and #16 (Cigarette lighter) in the Instrument panel fuse box.

Passenger Compartment
