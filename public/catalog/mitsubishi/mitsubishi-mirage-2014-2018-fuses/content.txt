In this article, we consider the sixth-generation Mitsubishi Mirage, produced from 2012 to 2024. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Mitsubishi Mirage 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019, 2020, 2021, 2022, 2023, and 2024, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Mitsubishi Mirage / Space Star 2014-2024








Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuse in the Mitsubishi Mirage is the fuse #2 in the Instrument panel fuse box.
