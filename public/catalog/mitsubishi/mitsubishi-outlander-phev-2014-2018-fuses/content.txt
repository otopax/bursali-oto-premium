Fuse Box Location
Passenger Compartment
Left-hand drive vehicles:
The fuse box is located in the instrument panel (on the driver’s side) to the left of the steering wheel, behind the cover. Pull the fuse lid to remove it.
A – Main fuse block;  B – Sub fuse block.
Right-hand drive vehicles:

A – Main fuse block.
Open the glove box, uncouple the rod (A) on the right side of the glove box; while pressing the side of the glove box, unhook the left and right hooks (B) and lower the glove box; remove the glove box fastener (C), and then remove the glove box.
  
B – Sub fuse block.
When changing the fuse of the sub fuse block, perform it with the hole of the bottom cover.

Engine Compartment

A – Main fuse block; B – Sub fuse block
