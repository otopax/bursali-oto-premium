In this article, we consider the first-generation Renault Sandero, produced from 2007 to 2012. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Renault Sandero 2012, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Renault Sandero I 2008-2012



Information from the owner’s manual of 2012 is used. The location and function of fuses in cars produced at other times may differ.
