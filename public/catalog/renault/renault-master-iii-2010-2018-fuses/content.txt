In this article, we consider the third-generation Renault Master, produced from 2010 to 2018. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Renault Master 2016, 2017 and 2018, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Renault Master III 2010-2018



Information from the owner’s manual of 2016, 2017 and 2018 is used. The location of fuses in cars produced at other times may differ.

Fuse Box in the Engine Compartment
Fuse Box Location

Removing:

Remove screw 2 and remove coolant reservoir 3.


Unscrew the four screws 4.


Turn unit 5 over to access the fuses in the engine compartment.


Refitting fuse box:

Clip the mountings 7, then clip mounting 6.


Screw the four screws 4 to ensure that unit 5 is completely sealed (Check that no water or dust enters unit 5 when it is being removed/refitted.).


Position coolant reservoir 3, then screw bolt 2.


Assignment of the fuses
To identify the fuses, refer to the fuse allocation label.
