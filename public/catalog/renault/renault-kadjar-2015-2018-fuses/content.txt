The compact SUV Renault Kadjar is available from 2015 to the present. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Renault Kadjar 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Box Location
Engine Compartment
The fuse box is located under the air intake duct.
Fuse Box №1 in the Passenger Compartment
It is located on the left-hand side of the dashboard.

Left-hand drive vehicles
Unclip the cover A using a screwdriver or similar.


Right-hand drive vehicles
Open the glovebox and unclip the flap B.


Fuse Box №2 in the Passenger Compartment
Fuses in passenger compartment C on the right-hand side of the dashboard
Unclip the cover C using a screwdriver or similar.

Certain fuses should only be replaced by a qualified professional. These fuses are not listed on the label. Only change the fuses represented on the label.
