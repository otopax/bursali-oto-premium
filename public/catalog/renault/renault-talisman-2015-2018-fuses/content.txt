In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Renault Talisman 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Box Location
The fuse box is located on the left of the steering wheel behind the cover.
Unclip the access flap A. Use the fuse allocation label in storage compartment A to identify the fuses.

Certain fuses should only be replaced by a qualified professional. These fuses are not listed on the label. Only change the fuses represented on the label.
