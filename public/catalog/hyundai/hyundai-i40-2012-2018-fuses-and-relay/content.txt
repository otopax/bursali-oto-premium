The Hyundai i40 is available from 2012 to the present. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai i40 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout) and relay.

Fuse Layout Hyundai i40 2012-2018




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Hyundai i40 are the fuses #1 “POWER OUTLET 2” (Console power outlet, Rear power outlet) and #6 “POWER OUTLET 1” (Front power outlet) in the Instrument panel fuse box.
