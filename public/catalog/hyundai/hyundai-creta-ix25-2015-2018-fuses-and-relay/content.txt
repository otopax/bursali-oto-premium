In this article, we consider the first-generation Hyundai Creta (ix25), produced from 2015 to 2020. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai Creta (ix25) 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout) and relay.

Fuse Layout Hyundai Creta / ix25 2015-2019…




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Hyundai Creta / ix25 are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses “C/LIGHTER” (Cigar lighter), “POWER OUTLET” (Front power outlet) and “POWER OUTLET” (Rear power outlet)).
