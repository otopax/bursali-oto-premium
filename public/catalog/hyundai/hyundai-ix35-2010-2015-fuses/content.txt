The compact crossover Hyundai ix35 was produced from 2010 to 2015. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai ix35 2010, 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014 and 2015, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Hyundai ix35 2010-2015




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses “POWER OUTLET 1” and “POWER OUTLET 2”).
