In this article, we consider the second-generation Hyundai i20, available from 2015 to the present. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai i20 2015 and 2016, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Information from the owner’s manuals of 2015 and 2016 is used. The location and function of fuses in cars produced at other times may differ.

Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Hyundai i20 are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses “POWER OUTLET LH” (Left socket) and “POWER OUTLET RH” (Right socket)).

Engine Compartment
The fuse box is located in the engine compartment (left-side).

Inside the fuse/relay panel covers, you can find the label describing fuse/relay name and capacity. Not all fuse panel descriptions in this manual may be applicable to your vehicle. It is accurate at the time of printing. When you inspect the fuse box on your vehicle, refer to the fusebox label.
