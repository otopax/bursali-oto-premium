The compact five-door liftback Hyundai Ioniq Electric is available from 2017. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai Ioniq Electric 2017, 2018 and 2019, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout) and relay.
