The mini MPV Hyundai ix20 was produced from 2011 to 2019. In this article, you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai ix20 2011, 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015, 2016 and 2017, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Hyundai ix20 2011-2019




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses in the Hyundai ix20 are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses “POWER OUTLET FRT” (Front power outlet) and “POWER OUTLET RR” (Rear and centre power outlets)).
