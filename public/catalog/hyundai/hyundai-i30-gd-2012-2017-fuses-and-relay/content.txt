In this article, we consider the second-generation Hyundai i30 (GD), produced from 2012 to 2017. Here you will find fuse box diagrams of Hyundai i30 2012, 2013, 2014, 2015 and 2016, get information about the location of the fuse panels inside the car, and learn about the assignment of each fuse (fuse layout).

Fuse Layout Hyundai i30 2012-2017




Cigar lighter (power outlet) fuses are located in the Instrument panel fuse box (see fuses #1 “CIGAR LIGHT” (console cigar lighter, rear power outlet) and #5 “POWER OUTLET” (front power outlet)).
